Our Services

Media Services

As a subsidiary under Destiny Holdings, Destiny Media Company specializes in the media industry. This company is engaged in the creation, production, and distribution of various media content, which include print, digital, audio, and video media. It also involve broadcasting, content creation, and advertising services.

Transport, Logistics and Travel

This subsidiary focuses on logistics, transportation, and travel-related services. It include freight and cargo transportation, travel agencies, and logistical support for various industries. Destiny Logistics and Travel plays a key role in facilitating the movement of goods and people efficiently.

Information Communication & Technology College

Destiny ICT College is an educational subsidiary focused on Information and Communication Technology. It provides education and training in various ICT disciplines, preparing students for careers in technology, software development, network management, and other related fields.

Marketing and Branding

Destiny Branding and Marketing is a subsidiary dedicated to providing branding and marketing solutions. This includes developing and managing brands, creating marketing campaigns, and offering advertising and promotional services to clients. The company helps businesses build their presence in the market

Technological Solutions

Under Destiny Holdings, Destiny Technologies is involved in the tech sector. This subsidiary could be engaged in various technology-related activities, such as software development, IT services, hardware solutions, and technological innovations. It may also include research and development initiatives.

Event Planning and Management.

Specializes in event planning and management services. This subsidiary is dedicated to conceptualizing, organizing, and executing a wide range of events, including conferences, conventions, trade shows, corporate meetings, product launches, weddings, and social gatherings.
